Safety & Compliance

Safety is not a document. It is how we operate.

CARE is committed to complying with all applicable NSW safety requirements for silica-generating works. For concrete cutting, coring, grinding and surface preparation, that means a disciplined approach to risk assessment, silica control measures, engineering controls, respiratory protection, worker training, air monitoring where required, health monitoring where applicable, and site housekeeping.

We believe safety and compliance must be built into the job from the outset, not added after the work starts.

SWMS & Risk Management

Safe Work Method Statements prepared for every scope, reviewed before work begins and updated as conditions change.

Licences & Certifications

All personnel hold current White Cards, relevant High Risk Work Licences and trade certifications required for the works.

Insurances & Documentation

Current public liability, workers compensation, and professional indemnity insurances maintained and available on request.

Practical technology supports clear communication, accurate reporting and efficient project delivery.
