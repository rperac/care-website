Services

Scanning, Coring, Cutting & Preparation

Coordinated site capability for investigation, access and substrate preparation ahead of repair works.

Concrete Scanning

Locate what is inside the element before work begins.

GPR and cover-meter scanning to locate reinforcement, post-tension cables, and embedded services before any cutting or coring begins. Prevents strikes, satisfies safe-work requirements, and gives the engineer a clear picture of what's inside the element.

Grinding & Preparation

Prepare the substrate to the specified profile.

Mechanical surface preparation including diamond grinding, scabbling, and hydro-prep to the profile and cleanliness standard specified. Proper substrate preparation is a critical factor in repair bond performance.

Concrete Coring

Controlled coring through reinforced and post-tensioned concrete.

Diamond coring through reinforced and post-tensioned concrete for service penetrations, drainage, and investigation holes. Diameter, depth, and location per the engineer's drawings, with GPR clearance completed beforehand.

Concrete Cutting

Accurate cuts to the drawing and site tolerance.

Controlled saw-cutting and wire-cutting for joint formation, panel removal, and structural access. Clean edges, accurate depths, no overcuts, executed to the dimensions and tolerances on the drawing.
