Concrete Repair

Repair spalling, delamination and concrete cancer.

Spalling, delamination, concrete cancer, carbonation-induced corrosion. Break out, treat reinforcement, reinstate, executed step-for-step to the engineer's repair methodology with full ITP documentation.

Crack Injection

Structural crack bonding and water ingress sealing.

Epoxy and polyurethane injection for structural crack bonding and water ingress sealing. Product selection, injection pressure, and port spacing per the specification.

Protective Coatings

Coating systems applied to specification.

Anti-carbonation coatings, waterproof membranes, and corrosion-inhibiting systems applied to the specified product and film thickness. Surface preparation, priming, and application all documented.

CFRP strengthening to engineer design.

Carbon fibre (CFRP) plate and fabric application for flexural and shear strengthening to the structural engineer's design. Surface preparation, adhesive application, and laminate placement per manufacturer and engineer requirements.
